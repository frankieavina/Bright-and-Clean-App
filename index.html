About Us

Welcome to Bright & Clean! My name is Maria Cortes, and I bring over 5 years of professional cleaning experience to every space I touch. After years of perfecting my skills with a private cleaning company, I decided to start Bright & Clean to offer a higher standard of service that truly makes a difference. My mission is simple: to create spotless, refreshing environments where you can thrive, whether it's your home, office, or any space in between. With a keen eye for detail, a passion for cleanliness, and a personal touch in every job, I ensure your space isn’t just clean—it’s bright, welcoming, and ready for you to enjoy. Let me handle the cleaning, so you can focus on what matters most, with the peace of mind that your space is in expert hands.
